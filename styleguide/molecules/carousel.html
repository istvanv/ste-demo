Carousel - with autoplay enabled

Use js-owl-carousel_autoplay class to enable autoplay.

Plus use js-owl-carousel_autoplay--fast (3000ms), js-owl-carousel_autoplay--medium (5000ms - this is default) or js-owl-carousel_autoplay--slow (7000ms)
to set one of the predefined transition timeout - i.e. delay before next slide. 


              <div class="owl-carousel js-owl-carousel owl-carousel--style_standard js-owl-carousel_autoplay js-owl-carousel_autoplay--slow">
                <div class="js-carousel__slide-element" style="max-height: 360px;"><img class="c-image c-image--fit c-image--fit_fill" src="./styleguide/assets/sg-placeholder-material-landscape.jpg"></div>
