Who We Are

We have 50 years of engineering experience, a presence in over 100 countries around the world and a thorough understanding of the aerospace, electronics, land systems and marine sectors.

As a result, ST Engineering is trusted and valued by the customers we serve. Innovation is at the very core of what we do. That is how we have been able to push the frontiers of technology and engineering possibilities. Whether at the depths of the oceans or in the vastness of outer space, our engineers are driven to create solutions that meet tomorrows challenges.
