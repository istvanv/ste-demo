Bellevue Hill is an established and prestigious residential suburb, located approximately five kilometres east of the Sydney CBD. Neighbouring suburbs include Double Bay, Point Piper and Rose Bay. With glorious views to the city and harbour, Bellevue Hill is characterised by beautiful established houses, a number of which are home to some of Australiaâ€™s wealthiest and most famous celebrities. The area is also characterised by upmarket, luxury apartments.

The loan for this Syndicate-Fund has been provided to the borrower to assist with financing a development of ten residential townhouses on a property in Bellevue Hill. The property is located in close proximity to a number of major arterial roads, which provide easy access to the Sydney CBD. Further, it boasts plentiful nearby public transport, local schools and multiple shopping facilities, which include the Double Bay shopping precinct and the Westfield Bondi Junction shopping complex.

Bondi Beach is approximately 1.5 kilometres east of the property and the Sydney Cricket Ground is approximately 4.5 kilometres to the west.

Surrounding development comprises a mix of older to modern apartment buildings and prestige dwellings, with parkland adjoining to west.

The proposed development is for a residential unit complex comprising a total of ten apartments, comprising three with two bedrooms, six with three bedrooms, and one with four bedrooms, built over six levels. The apartments will each feature high quality internal fixtures and fittings, commensurate with the location and target owner-occupier market. Parking is provided in the basement in the form of car stacker accommodation that allows each unit to access an individual stacker that can accommodate two cars.
