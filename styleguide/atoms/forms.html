Size

To change the size of inputs:
you can either use the modifier .c-form--size_XX (sm, md, lg) on the form or any parent tag of the target form elements.
OR apply .form__control--size_XX (sm, md, lg) directly to the element, eg class="form__control form__control--size_sm"
